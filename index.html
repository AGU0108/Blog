Places I Have Viseted



DisneyLand, Orlando

I visited DisneyLand in August 2019

Disney is a magical and awesome place to go for a vacation! In my opinion Magic Kingdom was the best park!! There is the castle and at night there is a show that is so amazing that when is your last day you are gonna crie! One tip I give you is that you need to pack in your backpack some lunches and water because you have to walk a lot! Another tip is that if you want to get in a lot of rides I recommend you to be at the park at 8:00am because you will probably make almost a 3 hour line just for a ride. One more thing you can not forget while you are there is to BUY MINNE MOUSE EARSSS!!



New York City

I visited NYC in December 2021

NYC is a really nice city!! There is a lot of turism you can visit!!! One of my favorite places was Time Square. The first advice I give you is that as Disney you also have to walk a lot!!! So since you have to walk a lot I recommend that you don't have to much weight. Another tip is that you ALWAYS have your MetroCard with you because you are going to be from train to train so you need you MetroCard for you to pay!! Last is if you want to explore Manhattan you have to have a GPS, why am I telling you this is because it was my own experience!! Trust me it was terrible when you have a dad who lived there 4o years ago and thinks he stills remember EVERYTHING!



Washington DC

I visited Washington at the end of December and at the beginning of January

Washington is a really calm and nice state to live!! I went to pass time with my family I haven't seen in years!! One tip is that if you are on a snowy day DO NOT GO OUTSIDE because its dangerous!! The most lovely season is december because if you go to some streets near a lake you can see some Geese flying away because it their season.
